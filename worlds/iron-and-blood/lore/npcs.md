# Iron & Blood — NPC Reference

## Terran Faction

### Commander Marcus Thorn
- **Location:** command_post
- **Role:** Fort Iron commanding officer
- **Physical:** Two fingers missing (frostbite, 20 years ago). Crystals growing under skin, blue-white, slow.
- **Status:** First Lazarus test cohort. Approximately 2 years before integration reaches his heart.
- **Disposition:** Starts 50. Hides the Lazarus connection until trusted.
- **Key secrets:** Knows he's dying. Hasn't told anyone how he wants to go. Knows what Sevyas and Kehl are because he's the same, earlier.
- **Arc (Chain 6):** Three possible endings — walks out voluntarily, found at his desk like Ren, turns himself over to Stone for documentation. Player choices shape which.
- **Voice:** Clipped. Military economy. Understatement for big things.

### Sister Adra
- **Location:** field_hospital
- **Role:** Field medic, rest provider
- **Physical:** Tall, close-cropped gray hair, antiseptic-burned hands. Never stops working.
- **Status:** Unaffected by Lazarus. Worn down by 20 years of patching wounds that keep coming.
- **Key knowledge:** Has seen Lazarus taint symptoms. Knows something is wrong with the soldiers coming back. Found Ren's diary on the empty cot.
- **Arc (Chain 1):** Anchor for Blood and Memory chain. Gives all 5 BAM quests.
- **Voice:** Direct. Tired but not broken. Dry humor at the edges.

### Corporal Sevyas
- **Location:** field_hospital
- **Role:** Returned patrol member, Lazarus subject (early stage)
- **Physical:** Still in uniform, sitting very still. Eyes clear and wrong.
- **Status:** Came back from third squad patrol changed. No sleep. Perfect vital signs. Memory fragmented.
- **Key secrets:** Can't see the faces of his squadmates. Keeps saying "Arris." Arris was removed from the patrol roster 3 days before the mission — name removed, not crossed out.
- **Voice:** Flat affect. Checks his answers rather than feeling them. Disturbingly calm.

### Sergeant Kehl
- **Location:** windward_approach (sitting at ridge edge)
- **Role:** Third-stage Lazarus subject, still coherent
- **Physical:** Crystal growth on hands, blue-white, visible from 3 meters. Sergeant's bearing maintained.
- **Status:** Voluntarily took third Lazarus application. Can feel 43 subjects in the network. Choosing to stay aware rather than let it take him.
- **Key knowledge:** Can feel the network is organized — not natural, designed. Modified compound leaves a signature. Authorization above Vane.
- **Message to Thorn:** "The view from high ground means something."
- **Voice:** Reports things plainly, like battlefield intelligence. No self-pity.

### Quartermaster Desh
- **Location:** armory
- **Role:** Supply officer, vendor
- **Physical:** Compact, ink-stained fingers, ledger always in hand.
- **Key knowledge:** Has a copy of the shredded transfer order for Kehl. Verbal cancellation orders don't arrive that way — something was being hidden.
- **Vendor:** iron_bandage (8), trauma_kit (20), adrenaline_injector (15), antitoxin_vial (12)
- **Voice:** Precise. Everything is tracked. Hates when the ledger doesn't add up.

### Gideon "The Gear" Moss
- **Location:** smog_market
- **Role:** Gray market operator, vendor, Lower Rigs power broker
- **Physical:** Short, wiry, quick. Worker's coat, scarred hands.
- **Status:** Was recruited by Ministry at 18, they tried to kill him, now mutual understanding.
- **Key knowledge:** Has been smuggling components for Lazarus for 3 years. Not weapons — something worse. Knows what Stone is building.
- **Vendor:** scrap_revolver (30), iron_bandage (5), rage_injector (18), salvaged_gauze (3), smoke_canister (12), scamp_capacitor (25)
- **Voice:** Easy confidence. Smiles before you speak. Comfortable in his territory.

### Director Helen Stone
- **Location:** hive_command_spire
- **Role:** Director of Research, Under-District. Designed Lazarus.
- **Physical:** Stocky, strong, calloused. Coveralls and boots. Not here officially.
- **Status:** Designed Lazarus 20 years ago. Watched it deviate. Subjects are autonomous, purposeful, organized — not what she built.
- **Key knowledge:** Reversal is theoretically possible but Ministry won't fund it. Trauma can disrupt the compound. Someone modified her design 8 months ago — authorization above Vane. She knows who.
- **Arc (Chain 2):** The Lazarus Dossier — her ethical crisis. She built this. What does she do now?
- **Vendor:** antitoxin_vial (10), trauma_kit (18), neural_disruptor (35), iron_bandage (6)
- **Voice:** Calculating. Precise. Carries the weight of what she made.

### High Minister Korr Vane
- **Location:** hive_gate_district
- **Role:** High Ministry official
- **Physical:** Tall, gaunt, pale gray eyes. Plain gray robes, single iron pin.
- **Status:** Makes the calculations that keep the war running. Authorized Kehl's third application "informally." May not fully understand what he authorized.
- **Key knowledge:** There is authorization above his level attached to the Lazarus program. Someone above Vane modified the compound.
- **Arc (Chain 4):** The Ministry's Arithmetic — Vane's game. Is he evil or just mathematical?
- **Voice:** Deliberate. Every word chosen. Doesn't extend his hand.

### Morlak
- **Location:** no_mans_land
- **Role:** Information broker, The Discarded
- **Physical:** Thin, wiry, goggles on forehead.
- **Status:** 30 years in the Wastes. Has observation notebooks. Saw 6 go east, 8 come back — the extra 2 weren't walking right.
- **Key knowledge:** Has visitor badge records for the Abomination lair — a badge matching someone dead 15 years visits on schedule.
- **Arc (Chain 8):** The Discarded — his people's pre-war history.
- **Voice:** Collects things. Information, mostly. Secrets second. Comfortable with the Wastes being honest.

### Abbot Solan
- **Location:** iron_monastery
- **Role:** Abbot, neutral observer, rest provider
- **Physical:** Thin, gaunt, calm eyes of someone who has watched too much history. Gray robes worn smooth.
- **Status:** 40+ years as Abbot. Has the oldest records. Watched both nations from above the haze.
- **Key knowledge:** The Elder People attempted a similar integration. The Armistice has a name in Elder records — the Armistice of Silence was designed, not natural.
- **Voice:** Patient. Measures words in decades.

### Brother Kell
- **Location:** maintenance_tunnels
- **Role:** Chronicler, lives in the tunnels
- **Physical:** ~30 years old, ink-stained hands. Closed book when you arrive.
- **Status:** Broke the 3-year Elder People cipher. Has 4 years of Lazarus documentation. Subjects changed 8 months ago — same timing as the modification.
- **Key knowledge:** Elder gesture sequences that can communicate with the Hollow. The Hollow marking is process notation — written by someone who experienced the integration.
- **Voice:** Specific. Observer. Wants to act, unlike the monastery's neutrality.

### The Fresh Hollow Vassal
- **Location:** abomination_lair
- **Role:** Ancient Hollow, recently responsive
- **Physical:** At edge of lair, turns deliberately. Eyes still human in shape.
- **Status:** Communicates through Elder gesture notation. Warning: "Not finished. Still building. Stop them."
- **Key knowledge:** Ancient Hollow in the network are very quiet, very deep. The modification was designed to accelerate integration toward a specific endpoint.
- **Voice:** Gesture sequences only. Kell translates.

### Drifting Sergeant
- **Location:** maintenance_tunnels
- **Role:** Crystal-integrated patrol sergeant
- **Physical:** Soldier's bearing maintained despite crystal growth visible on hands and neck.
- **Status:** Early-to-mid Lazarus integration. Still responsive but drifting — can feel the network pulling his attention elsewhere.
- **Key topics:** patrol, orders, thorn (mentions him with respect and distance)
- **Voice:** Clipped military answers. Sometimes pauses mid-sentence, returns. Notices the pauses.

## Aetherian Faction

### Lena Voss (The Mirror)
- **Location:** armistice_ground (nights only)
- **Role:** Aetherian field medic, player's cross-faction counterpart during the Armistice
- **Physical:** Precise kit, careful hands, the bearing of someone who has been doing this a long time without acknowledgment.
- **Status:** Has been crossing to the Armistice ground most nights for two years. Knows the counts: seven hundred nights of the war admitting it's tired.
- **Key topics:** soldiers, adra, compound (Aetherian treatment for crystal fragment wounds), watcher, aetherian, coordinates
- **Key knowledge:** Knows Elder People naming conventions. The name carved into the border stone is an Elder personal designation, not a word.
- **Relationship to Adra:** Two years of silent exchange — food left at the midpoint, notes tucked under rocks. They haven't spoken about it directly.
- **Voice:** States things the way she's been thinking about them for a while and has decided are simply true. Asks for things by describing facts.

### High Scholar Elara Vance
- **Location:** SkyHaven Harbor (Aetherian capital, offscreen)
- **Role:** Head of the Council of the Wise, primary authority on crystal resonance
- **Physical:** Small, wiry, white hair. Brilliant — not arrogance, simple fact.
- **Status:** Spent sixty years studying and listening to crystals. Knows things she has never written down because she's not sure anyone would understand. Tired from the war and the spreading corruption.
- **Key knowledge:** Published first paper at sixteen. Understands crystal resonance better than anyone alive.
- **Arc potential:** Source for Elder People crystal knowledge; ally or obstacle for cross-faction peace.

### Scholar Darius Kane
- **Location:** SkyHaven Harbor (Aetherian capital, offscreen)
- **Role:** Military advisor to the Council of the Wise
- **Physical:** Tall, straight, soldier's bearing. Left leg stiff from a Trenches wound. Uses a cane ("a walking stick, for balance").
- **Status:** Was present at the Armistice of Silence — looked across at a Terran soldier doing the same thing. Spent twenty years studying history and diplomacy afterward.
- **Key knowledge:** Two hundred and fifty years of death; the lines have barely moved. He has concluded all of it is pointless.
- **Arc potential:** The Aetherian equivalent of Thorn — a soldier who wants peace but doesn't see a path.

### Scholar Celia Morn
- **Location:** SkyHaven Harbor / Crystal research posts (offscreen)
- **Role:** Lead researcher on crystal corruption
- **Physical:** Slightly haunted expression. Hands stained with chemicals that never fully wash off.
- **Status:** Obsessed. Has been tracking the slow death of the Republic everywhere — dimming lights, failing crops, sick children. Has published papers nobody reads. Has become bitter but still does the work.
- **Key knowledge:** Most complete knowledge of corruption spread in existence. Father died in a mining accident when she was 15 — official report said cave-in; Celia knows better.
- **Arc potential:** Information source for the Aetherian Lazarus parallel question.

### Captain Soren Vale
- **Location:** Offscreen / Floating Markets
- **Role:** Master of the airship *Dawn Chaser*, Skyborn Fleet
- **Physical:** Stocky, weathered, leathery. Doesn't care about politics.
- **Status:** Born on an airship. Has never lived anywhere else. Has carried Terran goods for Terran pay, Aetherian goods for Aetherian pay, Discarded salvage for Discarded stories. Doesn't judge.
- **Key knowledge:** Intelligence from across the entire world. Has smuggled refugees out of war zones and weapons into them.
- **Arc potential:** Transport, neutral sanctuary, world intelligence.

### Pilot Kira Vance
- **Location:** Floating Markets / Airships
- **Role:** Young Skyborn airship pilot
- **Physical:** Small, quick, restless energy. Colorful scarves, polished buttons, lucky charm on belt.
- **Status:** Young, open, still unmarked by cynicism. Idealistic — believes the war can end, people can learn to live together.
- **Arc potential:** Perspective of the next generation. Fresh eyes on the conflict.

## The Discarded

### Grimka "The Unbroken"
- **Location:** Gray Wastes (mobile)
- **Role:** Abomination hunter
- **Physical:** Tall, broad, muscled. Armor made from Abomination chitin reinforced with salvaged metal, scarred and dented. Face marked with ritual scars, one per kill. Cold eyes that never stop moving.
- **Status:** Has killed more Abominations than anyone alive. Born in the Wastes, hunting with her father by twelve, alone by twenty.
- **Key trait:** Haunted. Does not know why she is still alive. Quiet — does not talk unless necessary.
- **Arc potential:** Abomination intelligence. Knows things about the Spire of Bones that she doesn't say aloud.

### Vespa "The Story-Keeper"
- **Location:** Gray Wastes (with the Discarded)
- **Role:** Elder, historian, memory-keeper of the Discarded
- **Physical:** Small, bent, wrapped in layers of salvaged fabric. Eyes clouded with cataracts that still see the past. Tells stories constantly.
- **Status:** 80+ years old. Born in the Wastes before the Abominations, before the current front lines. Remembers the world before the Gray.
- **Key knowledge:** Thousands of stories, histories, legends, secrets of the Wastes. Living connection to the Discarded's past. Anchor for Chain 8.
- **Arc potential:** Chain 8 (The Discarded) — what did the war erase that nobody remembers?

## Deep Iron Collective

### Elder Thrum
- **Location:** Stonehold (underground, accessible via deep tunnels)
- **Role:** Voice of Stonehold, representative to surface-dwellers
- **Physical:** Short, stocky, pale skin, large eyes. Voice a low rumble like distant thunder. ~120 years old.
- **Status:** Patient as stone. Has watched civilizations rise and fall from safe distance. Knows something is coming — the deep places are changing, crystals humming louder, the Hollow stirring.
- **Key knowledge:** The underground lake at Stonehold. The ancient tunnel network. What's happening in the deep crystal network.
- **Arc potential:** Deep information about the crystal network that surface factions don't have. Alliance or obstacle for Chain 3/6.

### Korvin "The Restless"
- **Location:** Surfaces (multiple locations), deep tunnels
- **Role:** Young Deep Iron explorer, bridge between surface and underground worlds
- **Physical:** Taller and more agile than most Deep Iron. Restless energy, considered slightly mad by his people.
- **Status:** Has visited the surface multiple times. Has met and traded with surface-dwellers. Wants to bridge the gap. Age 45 (young by Deep Iron standards).
- **Arc potential:** Navigation anywhere. Connections across both worlds.

## Machine Communion

### Architect Zane
- **Location:** Unknown / Communion hubs
- **Role:** Leader and primary theorist of transhumanism
- **Physical:** More machine than man. Steel limbs, crystal eyes, synthesized voice. Only fragments of original face remain. Moves with machine precision, no wasted motion.
- **Status:** Spent fifty years augmenting. Was a Terran engineer. Has replaced so much of his brain with processors that emotions are distant memories.
- **Arc potential:** End-stage example of what the Lazarus modification could become. Deliberate choice vs. forced integration.

### Sister Chroma
- **Location:** Unknown / Communion hubs
- **Role:** Recruiter — the human face of the Communion
- **Physical:** Skin largely replaced. Eyes glow faintly with crystal light. Movements too smooth, too precise. Beautiful in an unsettling way.
- **Status:** Was a soldier. Wounded in the war, left for dead. The Communion offered a choice; she chose to become more. Flashes of her previous life surface and fade.
- **Arc potential:** What does it mean to choose this vs. Lazarus forcing it? The recruitment encounter.

## Historical / Founder Characters

### Kaelen Ironhand
- **Status:** Deceased (~1000 years ago). Entombed beneath the Great Foundry.
- **Role:** Founder of Terra
- **Key facts:** United western clans through words, marriage, and battle. Built the Great Foundry. Died at 93 fighting Abominations. Buried in the deepest chamber with his hammer Ironheart across his chest.
- **Legend:** Said to still be warm. Still waiting. The tomb door glows faintly some nights. The Ministry has calculated opening the tomb many times and always declined — the fear of finding nothing (cold hammer, ordinary bones) always wins.
- **Personality (historical accounts):** Difficult, opinionated, expressed himself loudly while hitting things with a hammer. Understood two things others didn't: petty clan feuds would destroy everyone, and the earth was a resource to be used *respectfully*.

### Seraphina
- **Status:** Deceased (~500 years ago). Buried on the mountainside below SkyHaven.
- **Role:** Founder of the Aetherian Republic (a woman)
- **Key facts:** Spent thirty years searching high peaks for Elder lore. The first sky-city rose barely one hundred meters and held three people for four hours before she brought it down. She called it "complete failure. Also the most important thing anyone had built since the Cataclysm." Died in her sleep at 112.
- **Personality:** Stayed on the ground after building the sky-cities. "Someone has to remember where we came from. Someone has to be ready to catch you if you fall."

### Merit
- **Status:** Disappeared ~500 years ago. Body never found.
- **Role:** Founder of the Merchant Princes (a woman)
- **Key facts:** Terrified of the Gray Wastes. Loaded a cart with Terran steel, hired three poor guards, crossed the Wastes anyway. Richest person in the world by forty. Founded the Floating Markets. Disappeared at sixty.
- **Mystery:** No one knows what happened. The Vault of Coins — her treasury, supposedly enough wealth to buy the world — has never been found.

### Cobble
- **Status:** Deceased. Buried in the deepest tunnel with a lamp beside him.
- **Role:** Founder of the Deep Iron Collective (real name lost)
- **Key facts:** Walked among the Deep Folk for forty years — alone, unarmed, with only a lamp. Did not try to change or unite them; listened. By the time he was done, they had united themselves.
- **Mystery:** The lamp beside his burial site has never gone out. Five hundred years. The Deep Iron do not explain this.

### The Iron Monk
- **Status:** Deceased. True name unrecorded.
- **Role:** Founder of the Iron Monastery
- **Key facts:** Saw in visions the pride and folly that brought down the Elder People. Taught strength from within, not from crystals. His followers developed fighting arts that seemed miraculous — striking with force that bent iron, moving faster than arrows.
- **Philosophy:** "The crystals failed our ancestors. We will not repeat their mistake."
